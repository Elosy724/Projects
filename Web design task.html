Artificial Intelligence

AI,orArtificial Intelligence, is a branch of computer sciencethat focuses on
creating systems that can perform tasks that would normally require human levels of intelligence.AI is a wide discipline with numerous subfields, each with unique
objectives and specilizations.

AI can be categorized into three levels based on its capabilities:

Types of Intelligence
Type of AI Description
Artificial Narrow Intelligence(ANI): Designed to perform a single task
Artificial General Intelligence(AGI) Able to understand, learn, adapt and implement knowledge across a wide range of tasks at a human level
Artificial Super Intelligence(ASI) Refers to a future scenario whereAI surpasses human intelligence in nearly all economically valuable work


Difference between Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Data Science

  1. Artificial Intelligence refers to a computer systems that can behave intelligently, reason
    and learn like humans.

  2. Machine Learning is a subset of artificial intelligence focused on developing
    algorithms with the ability to learn without explicity being programmed

  3. Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning whose algorithms are inspired by the
    brain's structure and work exceptionally well with unstructured data such as images,
    videos, or text

  4. Data Science is a cross-disciplinary field that uses all of the above, amongst other
    skills like data analysis, statistics, data visualization, and more, to get insight from data.
